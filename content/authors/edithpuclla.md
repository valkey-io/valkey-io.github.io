Edith Puclla is Percona’s Technical Educator for Valkey, where she creates technical content, videos, demos, and learning resources to help users and the community better understand Valkey, Redis-compatible technologies, and open source data infrastructure.

Originally from Peru and now living in the United Kingdom, Edith studied Computer Engineering and has worked across software development, DevOps, and technology education. She is a CNCF Ambassador and Docker Captain, and has supported open source communities for more than seven years through talks, mentoring, content creation, event organization, documentation, and community initiatives.

Outside of work, Edith enjoys running and loves to spend time with her family!
