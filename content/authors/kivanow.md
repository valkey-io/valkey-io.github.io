Kristiyan Ivanov is the founder of BetterDB, a Valkey-native observability platform. He previously served as Engineering Manager at Redis, where he led Redis Insight, Redis for VS Code, and other developer tools. He has contributed to several Valkey repositories, including Valkey Admin.
