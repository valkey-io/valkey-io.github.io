Dragos Andriciuc is Percona's Technical Educator for Valkey where he creates technical content, blogs, and upstream documentation to help users and the community better understand Valkey. A Romanian national based in a small town outside of the capital of Bucharest, he studied Computer Science at Sheffield University and started to work in the technical writing field. He enjoys scuba diving, practicing airsoft, and spending time with his family.
