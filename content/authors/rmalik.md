Rameez Malik is a Master of Computer Science student at North Carolina State University, where he also earned his Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. He has experience as an AI Automation Intern at Axiom Workspace, a CRM-focused startup building an all-in-one alternative to modern enterprise platforms. There, he led the integration of AI into enterprise workflows and developed microservices within a .NET ecosystem that power LLM-driven web search automation. Outside of academics, he enjoys intellectual conversations that lie at the intersection of biotechnology and startups, with a dedicated passion for fitness.
